YOUNG TIGER NTR

Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr. (born 20 May 1983), popularly known by the initialism Jr NTR, is an Indian actor who primarily works in Telugu cinema. He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2012.Referred to in the media as the "Man of Masses", he has starred in 30 films. Rao's accolades include three Filmfare Awards, two Nandi Awards, two Siima Awards, four CineMAA Awards and one IIFA Award.
